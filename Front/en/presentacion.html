About me

Hello, I am Natalia, a passionate tattoo artist from Santiago del Estero. Since I started my career in 2018, I have been immersed in the world of tattooing, exploring and perfecting my technique. My passion for art has led me to create unique and personalized tattoos that reflect the essence of each person. Today, I feel deeply happy and fulfilled with what I do, and each design is a new creative adventure.
