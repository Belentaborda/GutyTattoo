About me

Hello! I'm Guty, an Argentinian tattoo artist with more than 5 years of experience in the world of body art. Each tattoo I make is a unique creation, carefully designed to reflect the essence of each person. My passion for the botanical world is reflected in each stroke and shadow that I apply with precision and dedication, capturing the beauty and vitality of nature. I have had the privilege of collaborating with colleagues, capturing my designs characterized by their unique and detailed style. My goal is to create not just a tattoo, but a meaningful and lasting experience for each person who trusts me to decorate their skin. Thank you for visiting my page and I hope to meet you soon!

Designs

My focus is on creating botanical tattoos, where I use shadows and blacks to capture the natural essence and beauty of flora on the skin.

Techniques

Shading with gradients

Creates smooth transitions between shades of black and grey, giving depth and volume to botanical shapes.

Fine and detailed lines

It involves tracing leaves and flowers with fine needles to capture the precise and natural details of the plants.
